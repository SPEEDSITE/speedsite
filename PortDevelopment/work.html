What we did.

Website Design & Development

Alligator Lawn Care had no real online presence: a basic site that loaded slowly, looked generic on mobile, and gave visitors no clear reason to call. We rebuilt it from scratch: a fast, clean website with dedicated service pages, location-specific copy, and a conversion-focused layout that puts the phone number and contact form front and center. Every design decision was made with one question in mind: does this help a local visitor become a lead?

Local SEO & GBP Optimization

Google Business Profile is the single most valuable asset for a local service business, and most businesses leave it almost completely untouched. We audited and rebuilt Alligator's GBP from the ground up: accurate service area definitions, keyword-rich business descriptions, a structured photo strategy, and consistent NAP data across the web. Combined with on-site local SEO, the result was a 300% spike in profile interactions and a second-place ranking on Google within the first month of going live.

Review Generation

Reviews are both social proof and a direct ranking signal. We implemented a simple, low-friction review generation process, a system that made it easy for Alligator's satisfied customers to leave honest feedback on Google. Within 30 days, their review count had doubled. More reviews meant higher trust, better rankings, and stronger visibility on AI platforms like ChatGPT and Perplexity, which increasingly pull from verified local business data when recommending service providers.
