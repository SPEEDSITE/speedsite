About Port Digital

We are
Port Digital.

Port Digital exists to give local businesses the digital edge they deserve: fast, focused, and built entirely around results.

Who We Are

Port Digital is a digital growth agency built specifically for local service businesses. We're not a generalist agency. We don't do everything for everyone. We do one thing: build the digital systems that help local businesses get found, get leads, and grow.

That focus is deliberate. When you only serve one type of client, you get very good at it very quickly. Every website we've built, every Google Business Profile we've optimized, and every lead system we've deployed has been for a local service business. That's not a constraint. It's a competitive advantage.

What Drives Us

We started because we saw a gap: talented tradespeople and service providers who were excellent at what they did, yet completely invisible online. The big agencies were too expensive, the cheap builders were too generic, and local business owners were stuck between bad options.

We exist to fix that. Every business we work with is run by someone who shows up early, does good work, and deserves a steady flow of customers. Our job is to make sure those customers can find them.

Who We Work With

We work with local service businesses: lawn care, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, cleaning, contracting, and anyone who depends on local customers to fill their calendar. If you're great at your trade and want more people to find you, we're the team to call.

We keep our client list intentionally small so that every engagement gets our full attention. We're not farming your project out to a junior team. You work directly with us.
